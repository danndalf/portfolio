The Silk Road was a Burning Man honorarium art installation led by Julia Logan Trimarco. I worked on the structural engineering, designed and built the nighttime lighting electronics and power grid, and helped install it next to the temple at Burning Man 2014.

The installation was a series of arches in the architectural styles of each major city along the ancient silk road. After the festival, the wood was reclaimed to build a house, and the electronics were recycled into other art projects.
